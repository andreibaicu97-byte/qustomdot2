Who is QustomDot?

QustomDot is a deep-tech company specializing in quantum dot materials for next-generation displays. Founded as a spin-off from Ghent University, our technology is the result of over two decades of research in quantum dot synthesis and surface engineering.

Our mission is to bring quantum dot color to every screen — sustainably, without toxic materials, and without complexity. We develop cadmium-free quantum dot inkjet inks that can be applied directly on-chip — removing the barriers that have kept quantum dots out of microLED and other advanced display applications.
