Meet QustomDot

Display scientists, ink chemists, and process engineers — making Cd-free Quantum Dot color conversion production-ready, from material synthesis to inkjet formulation.

Co-Engineered

We engineer the full system — not just the ink

Other suppliers start with off-the-shelf quantum dots and adapt them into an ink. We co-engineer the QD surface and the ink formulation together from the start — so every batch is optimized for direct on-chip integration.

Chip-Ready

Device-ready, out of the box

QD materials alone can't go directly onto a chip. QustomJet™ is formulated specifically for inkjet deposition — no extra processing, no physical separation from the light source, no workarounds.

IP-Protected

Built on protected science

Our surface engineering methods, ligand library, and QustomJet™ Gen1 & Gen2 formulations are patent-protected. When you build with QustomDot, you build on a defensible technology foundation.
